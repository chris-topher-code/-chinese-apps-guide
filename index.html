Quick comparisons

Which one should I use?

WeChat Pay vs Alipay

Use both if possible. WeChat Pay is everywhere socially; Alipay is often smoother for travel, utilities, and foreign card setup.

Taobao vs JD vs Pinduoduo

Taobao has everything, JD is stronger for reliable electronics and delivery, Pinduoduo is deal-heavy but harder for beginners.

Amap vs Didi

Amap is the map and transit brain. Didi is the ride-hailing backup when public transport is not enough.
